  • OVERVIEW

    I have built a website for Hair Space Reile, a men's salon in Japan.

01

CONCEPT

I tried to create a stylish atmosphere with a Western feel, rather than the inorganic atmosphere of a typical hair salon. For the key visuals, I photographed the tools and hair styling products that I usually use, and adjusted them to look like a magazine while creating a sense of commitment and craftsmanship. The main color is a slightly darker floor in the store, and is adjusted so that there is no difference in atmosphere from the inside of the store.

02

DEVELOPMENT

Regarding coding, we do not reinvent the wheel, but utilize a simple plug-in. AOS is used for the scroll animation of fade-in, and instafeed.js is used for getting Instagram posts. Wordpress is used for the CMS, and you can update your blog using your smartphone.
